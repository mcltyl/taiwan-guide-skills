### Night Market Guide

| Market | Location | Known For | Tips |
|--------|----------|-----------|------|
| Shilin 士林 | Taipei | Biggest, most famous | Touristy, go late |

## Ordering & Etiquette

### How to Order

**Night Markets:**
1. Point at what you want
2. Say number: 一個 (yī gè) = one, 兩個 (liǎng gè) = two
3. 內用 (nèi yòng) = eat here, 外帶 (wài dài) = takeaway
4. Pay cash (many don't take cards)

**Restaurants:**
1. Often self-seating
2. Write order on paper slip
3. Or point at menu
4. 買單 (mǎi dān) = bill please

### Useful Phrases

| Chinese | Pinyin | Meaning |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 這個 | zhè gè | this one |
| 多少錢 | duō shǎo qián | how much |
| 不要辣 | bù yào là | no spicy |
| 少辣 | shǎo là | less spicy |
| 不要香菜 | bù yào xiāng cài | no cilantro |
| 加蛋 | jiā dàn | add egg |
| 打包 | dǎ bāo | to go / pack it |
| 好吃 | hǎo chī | delicious |

### Dining Culture

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Share dishes family-style | Tip (not expected) |
| Slurp noodles (it's fine) | Stick chopsticks upright in rice |
| Say 謝謝 when served | Leave food uneaten (waste) |
| Try everything | Be picky publicly |

## Dietary Restrictions

### Vegetarian (素食)

Taiwan is vegetarian-friendly (Buddhist influence).

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| 全素 | Strict vegan (no garlic/onion) |
| 蛋奶素 | Lacto-ovo vegetarian |
| 五辛素 | Vegan but with garlic/onion OK |

**Look for:** 素食 signs, Buddhist restaurants near temples

**⚠️ Warning:** "Vegetable" dishes may have meat stock or oyster sauce. Always ask: 有肉嗎？(yǒu ròu ma?) = Is there meat?

### Halal

Growing halal options in cities. Search: 清真 (qīng zhēn)

### Allergies

| Allergen | Chinese |
|----------|---------|
| Peanuts | 花生 |
| Shellfish | 海鮮/貝類 |
| Gluten | 麩質 |
| Soy | 大豆 |

Say: 我對 [allergen] 過敏 (wǒ duì [X] guò mǐn) = I'm allergic to [X]

## Food Safety

Taiwan has high food safety standards, but:

- Drink tap water? **No.** Buy bottled or use filtered.
- Ice in drinks? **Usually safe** in established shops.
- Street food? **Generally safe.** Look for busy stalls.
- Raw food? **Sashimi OK** at reputable places.
