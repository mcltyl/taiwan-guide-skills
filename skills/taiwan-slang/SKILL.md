# TAIWANESE MANDARIN

## Pronunciation Differences

Taiwan Mandarin differs from China Mandarin:

| Feature | Taiwan | China |
|---------|--------|-------|
| **Retroflex** | Softer (sh→s) | Strong |
| **R sound** | Often dropped | Standard |
| **Tone** | More melodic | Flatter |
| **和** | hàn | hé |
| **什麼** | shé me | shén me |

| **2486** | Strong insult |

## Generational

- Older people may not understand internet slang
- Young people may cringe at outdated terms
