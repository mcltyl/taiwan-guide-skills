# MEDIA LANDSCAPE

## Key Concept: Pan-Blue vs Pan-Green

Taiwan's media is heavily influenced by political alignment:

| Camp | Political Leaning | Generally Supports |
|------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Pan-Blue 泛藍** | KMT, closer China ties | KMT, TPP |
| **Pan-Green 泛綠** | DPP, Taiwan independence | DPP |

**⚠️ Almost all Taiwan media has a lean. Read multiple sources.**

## Reading Bias

### How to Detect Lean

**Pan-Blue indicators:**
- Emphasizes cross-strait economic ties
- Critical of DPP policies
- Uses "mainland" (大陸) for China
- More coverage of KMT activities

**Pan-Green indicators:**
- Emphasizes Taiwan sovereignty
- Critical of China
- Uses "China" (中國) not "mainland"
- More coverage of DPP achievements

**Neutral indicators:**
- Presents multiple viewpoints
- Fact-focused, less commentary
- Transparent about sources

### Verification Tips

1. **Check multiple sources** — If only one outlet reports it, be skeptical
2. **Look for primary sources** — Government statements, court documents
3. **Check the date** — Old news recirculates
4. **Reverse image search** — Verify photos
5. **Search forums** — `site:ptt.cc [新聞標題]` for local discussion

## Sensitive Topics

These topics are especially prone to bias:

| Topic | Blue Spin | Green Spin |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| **Cross-strait relations** | Emphasize economic benefit | Emphasize security threat |
| **US-Taiwan relations** | Caution about provoking China | Celebrate support |
| **COVID response** | Critical of DPP handling | Praise Taiwan's success |
| **Economy** | Blame DPP policies | Blame global factors |
| **Energy policy** | Pro-nuclear | Anti-nuclear |

## Misinformation Landscape

### Common Sources of Disinfo

- **Content farms** — Clickbait sites copying/distorting news
- **LINE groups** — Forwarded messages, often false
- **Facebook** — Older demographics, more disinfo
- **YouTube** — Political commentary channels
- **China-origin** — Disinformation campaigns

### Fact-Checking Resources

| Resource | Chinese | What It Does |
|----------|---------|--------------|
| **Taiwan FactCheck Center** | 台灣事實查核中心 | Independent fact-checker |
| **MyGoPen** | MyGoPen | Debunks LINE rumors |
| **Cofacts** | Cofacts | Crowdsourced fact-check |
| **DoubleThink Lab** | 台灣民主實驗室 | Tracks disinformation |

**Always verify** sensational claims, especially about:
- China threats
- Political scandals
- Health/medical claims
- Election-related news

## Getting News

### Recommended Approach

1. **Daily overview:** Focus Taiwan (CNA) for facts
2. **In-depth:** The Reporter (報導者) for investigations
3. **Multiple perspectives:** Read one Blue + one Green source
4. **International context:** Reuters, AP for outside view
5. **Local discussion:** PTT for public sentiment

### News Aggregation

| Method | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Google News Taiwan** | news.google.com/taiwan |
| **PTT News板** | ptt.cc/bbs/news |
| **Podcast** | 報導者 Podcast, 轉角國際 |

### Social Media Caution

- **PTT** — Good for sentiment, but anonymous = unverified
- **Facebook** — Older users, more disinfo
- **Twitter/X** — Tech/politics crowd, some good accounts
- **Threads** — Growing, younger demographic

```

```

## Red Flags in Taiwan News

Be skeptical if you see:

- 🚩 "Anonymous source" with no verification
- 🚩 Extreme claims with no evidence
- 🚩 Only one outlet reporting
- 🚩 Emotional language, no facts
- 🚩 Old news presented as new
- 🚩 Screenshots instead of links
- 🚩 "Share before they delete this!"
